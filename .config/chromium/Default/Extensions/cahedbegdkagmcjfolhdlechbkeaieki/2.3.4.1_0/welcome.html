Chromoji - Emoji for Google Chrome™

Hey! You've just gained access to all the awesome emojis around the web.

How does it work❓

This extension let's you view emojis posted on websites, forums, chat boxes and more.
If you can see these, you're good to go: 😄😃😀😊☺️😉😍😘😚😗😙😜😝😛😳😁😔😘

Insert your emojis❗

A toolbar button was added to your browser so you can access emojis anywhere you go.
An example to copy&paste emojis:
1) *
2) Click the toolbar button 🚀 in the top right corner of your browser
3) Click an emoji inside the new box that opened
* If you don't have any text box selected, the emoji will be copied to your clipboard
