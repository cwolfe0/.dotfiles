What is Heartrate Reserve?

Heart Rate Reserve (HRR) is the difference between your Maximum Heart Rate (Max HR) and Resting Heart Rate (Rest HR). This is basically your global heart capacity :)

When %HRR is 0% then your Rest HR equals {{userRestHr}}
When %HRR is 100% then your Max HR equals {{userMaxHr}}

What are Heart Rate Reserve Zones?

Heart Rate Reserve Zones represent a distribution of your heart efforts during an activity into zones. Each zone has a beginning and ending HRR value.

Where to see HRR Zone distribution?

Inside a Strava activity. Of course you must have heart rate data associated to your activity.

Edit existing zone

Click into HRR fields of the zone you want to edit. A zone is always defined between 2 HRR fields.

Hint: You can use input arrows or keyboard arrows to set the value.

Add a Last Heartrate Reserve Zone

Click add button to add a new zone at the end of current zones. The previous last zone will be split twice to add the new zone.

Remove Heartrate Reserve Zone

Click delete button to remove the zone at the end of current zones. The 2 latest zones will be merged to perform this operation.

Reset Heartrate Reserve Zones

Click refresh to apply default factory zones.

Save Heartrate Reserve Zones

Click save to save zones
